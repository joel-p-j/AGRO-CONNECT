In the agriculture sector, customers play a pivotal role as their purchasing decisions directly influence the types of crops grown, farming practices employed, and overall market direction, essentially acting as the driving force behind production choices; by expressing their preferences for quality, sustainability, and specific produce, consumers can shape the agricultural landscape, encouraging farmers to adapt and innovate to meet evolving demands, thus contributing to a dynamic and responsive food system. Consumers have significant influence over the direction of the food and agriculture system. Through their purchases they express their preferences and values and help shape the decisions producers and retailers make.
